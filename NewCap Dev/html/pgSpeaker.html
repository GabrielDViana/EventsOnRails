Speaker

Imagine if you could teach anywhere in the world
without restraints...

Set your project, set the place and do it!

Become a Speaker with NewCap and help knowledge connect people all over the world. The basic idea is that anyone has something to teach and there are tons of people willing to learn. Every time people is discussing some subject or even just chatting, they are learning and retaining knowledge. So why can't we expand this concept giving people the opportunity to talk or teach not necessarily in school or a classroom, but anywhere they think it would be better?

A human being has 7 intelligences that can be exercised in many different ways. When we are walking down the street we are processing tons of informations and many times learning by doing. NewCap brings a platform that enables you to give a talk or a class to Attendees and be paid for that. You just need to create your project giving a syllabus of the topic you want to talk about, set the place and time, set how much each Attendee has to pay and make it happen! You don't need to have a degree or be linked to any academic institution. At the end of the week you will receive the payment for your work and will receive a working hours report.

Let's change the way knowledge is seen and given!

Build your project

Create your project or disscussion topic in an easy and powerful way. Set recommended prior knowledge and resources so that attendees can study before.

Set the place

Is a classroom the best place to learn? If yes, set it there. If not, choose the place that suits you best. A camp, a farm, a museum or anywhere that you think will maximize everybodies experience.

Set your price

Choose if you want to do it for free or if you want to get paid. Time is a precious thing and we got to make it count. Set the minimmum number of Attendees and how much it will cost your talk per Attendee.
