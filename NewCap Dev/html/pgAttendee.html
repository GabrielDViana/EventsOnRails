Attendee

Imagine if you could learn anywhere in the world
without restraints...

Choose the project, choose the place and do it!

Become a Attendee with NewCap and learn with other people all over the world. The basic idea is that anyone has something to teach and there are tons of people willing to learn. Every time people is discussing some subject or even just chatting, they are learning and retaining knowledge. So why can't we expand this concept giving people the opportunity to talk or teach not necessarily in school or a classroom, but anywhere they think it would be better?

A human being has 7 intelligences that can be exercised in many different ways. When we are walking down the street we are processing tons of informations and many times learning by doing. NewCap brings a platform that enables you to participate at a talk or a class in different places that will maximize your experience of learning. You just need to choose the project by its syllabus, by the place and time that it will occur, by the amount you have to pay and make it happen! At the end of the class, rate your Speaker and let others know if it is worth it or not. Recognize the good ones and make the bad ones lower their prices or talk about something they are better!

You don't need to have a degree or be linked to any academic institution. At the end of the week you will receive a learning hours report and a certificate of the classes or discussions you`ve been.

Let's change the way knowledge is seen and given!
