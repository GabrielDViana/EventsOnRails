NewCap

"Connecting people through knowledge"

Founded in November 2015 and based in Brasília, Brazil, NewCap was created with the purpose of making knowledge get to any part of the world. It is a platform that enables people who wants to teach or discuss subjects connect with people that wants to learn. Doesn´t matter if you are a Nobel Prize winner, a graduate student or a non degree person. Anyone can teach something to other person that is willing to learn. Speakers can schedule talks and classes anywhere in the world and Attendees can easily know when and where it is going to take place. Nevertheless, it makes it possible to fund personal projects even if you are not going to be at the talk. Spread knowledge!
